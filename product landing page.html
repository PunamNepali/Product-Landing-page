Features

  • Exfoliation: Removes dead skin cells and unclogs pores by dissolving oil and debris.
  • Anti-Inflammatory: Reduces redness and inflammation associated with acne.
  • Anti-Bacterial: Fights acne-causing bacteria and reduces the risk of new breakouts.
  • Oil Control: Penetrates deep into pores, dissolves excess oil, and regulates sebum production.
  • Gentle Yet Effective: Acts as a mild exfoliant suitable for sensitive and non-sensitive skin.
  • Keratinization: Breaks down keratin buildup to prevent blackheads and whiteheads.
  • Improves Skin Texture: Makes skin smoother and reduces the appearance of enlarged pores.
  • Comedolytic Properties: Prevents the formation of blackheads and whiteheads by reducing pore blockages.

How It Works

  1. Penetrates Deep Into Pores: Salicylic acid is oil-soluble, allowing it to penetrate through the oily layer on the skin and reach deep into the pores. This helps target clogged pores and blackheads.
  2. Exfoliates Dead Skin Cells: It weakens the bonds (desmosomes) between dead skin cells, enabling gentle exfoliation and preventing pore blockages and keratin buildup.
  3. Clears Excess Oil (Sebum): Dissolves excess oil inside the pores, reducing oiliness and minimizing breakouts.
  4. Reduces Inflammation: Its anti-inflammatory properties soothe redness and swelling, calming the skin and reducing the appearance of inflamed acne.
  5. Prevents Bacterial Growth: By keeping the pores clean and free of oil and debris, salicylic acid reduces the growth of acne-causing bacteria (*Propionibacterium acnes*).
  6. Prevents Blackheads and Whiteheads: It dissolves the plug of dead skin cells and oil in clogged pores, preventing the formation of blackheads and whiteheads.
  7. Improves Skin Texture: Removes dead skin cells and unclogs pores, resulting in smoother skin and improved overall clarity.
